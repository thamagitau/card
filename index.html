CREATIVITY, INNOVATION, AND INVENTION

CREATIVITY

Creativity is the ability to think about a task or a problem in a new or different way, or the ability to use the imagination to generate new ideas.

INNOVATION

Innovation is the practical implementation of ideas that lead to the introduction of new products or services or the improvement of the provision of products or services.

INVENTION

Invention is the act of bringing ideas or objects together in a novel way to create something that did not exist before.
